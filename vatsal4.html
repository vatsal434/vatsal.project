Space Exploration: Journey Beyond Earth

Space is a fascinating frontier. Here are some key milestones in space exploration:

  1. 1957: Sputnik 1 became the first artificial satellite.
  2. 1969: Neil Armstrong walked on the Moon during the Apollo 11 mission.
  3. 1990: The Hubble Space Telescope was launched, giving us incredible images of the universe.

Learn more at NASA's website.
